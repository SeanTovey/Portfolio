About Me
Hi! I'm Sean, I make games and interactive experiences using 3D modeling and sometimes through websites. I focus mostly on 3D modeling but I often use skills from other fields to create something that lines up with my vision. I am from the US and am currently a third year student at RMIT studying for a Bachelor of Design in Digital Media. Here you can see some of the projects I made over the course of my three years. I have an interest in narrative focused games and I enjoy creating new worlds with atmospheric environments. I draw a lot of inspiration from older role playing games and want to capture the feeling of adventure and mystery I get from them in my works. I love learning new skills and collaborating with people from different disciplines to create projects I can’t do alone. My favorite projects are ones that require me to think outside the box in order to deliver an immersive experience. I care deeply about the future and many of my projects speculate on how today’s technology and beliefs will affect the future of humanity. Feel free to check out my socials or contact me at my email address if you want to collaborate.
